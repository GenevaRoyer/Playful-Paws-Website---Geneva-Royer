How to Adopt Your Pet

Benefits of Adopting from Shelters and Rescues [1]

  • There is a 25% chance of adopting a purebred.
  • The cost of adopting a pet is much cheaper than buying one in your local pet stores. Occasionally, you purchase a pet for free at a shelter or rescue.
  • You decrease the number of animals in shelters. Shelters house 6â€“8 million animals annually mainly because their respective family moved or landlord prohibited them from the property. Approximately, half of them get adopted.
  • Workers from shelters and rescues give families behavioral analyses and advices for each pet to give the family and the pet the best match.

How to Adopt from Shelters and Rescues [1]

  1. Do research!
  2. Do not rush. Patience leads to a better match.
  3. Visit local shelters and rescues. Shelter Pet Project may not list all of the shelters and rescues nearby.
